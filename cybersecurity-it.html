Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cybersecurity and IT services

Is penetration testing legal?
Yes — when conducted with written authorization from the system owner. We always begin with a formal scope agreement that defines the targets, testing boundaries, and rules of engagement. Ethical hacking with proper authorization is fully legal and is standard practice worldwide.
How long does a penetration test take?
It depends on scope. A basic web application test typically takes 2–5 days. A full infrastructure assessment can take 1–3 weeks. We provide a clear timeline and milestone plan before starting any engagement.
Will the testing affect my live systems?
We take every precaution to minimize disruption. Testing is conducted carefully with agreed-upon safe hours, and we coordinate with your team throughout. In most cases, systems continue operating normally during the engagement.
What does the final report include?
Our reports include an executive summary for stakeholders, a full technical breakdown of all findings, CVSS risk ratings for each vulnerability, proof-of-concept evidence, and step-by-step remediation recommendations. We also offer a debrief call to walk through the findings.
Do you offer ongoing security monitoring?
Yes. Our Enterprise package includes continuous threat monitoring, quarterly assessments, and a dedicated security advisor. We also offer standalone monitoring retainers for businesses that need ongoing protection between formal assessments.
